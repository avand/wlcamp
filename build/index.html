Do Something. This Summer!

At our overnight summer camp, we believe that the best way for boys to learn is through being active, especially while surrounded by natural beauty.

Our well-balanced summer camp program offers instruction in diverse activities, such as Archery, Waterskiing, and Wilderness Trips, just to name a few of our activities!

With our 100 years of experience, you can feel confident that your child will be safe as he has a summer which he will always remember — full of fun and learning new skills!
