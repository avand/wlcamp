Evening Program

Ever played capture the flag through the woods over a 250 acre camp? Are you itching to find out just what Mafia Night is? Or Counselor Hunt? Well you just have to wait until after dinner, when we gather for some of the most epic competitions that you could ever imagine. Actually, we dare you to think of something more epic than our E.P.’s. And if you can, just let us know. We’ll play it!
