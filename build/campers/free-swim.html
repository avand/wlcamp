Free Swim

Do what you want. No, seriously, anything you want to: go swimming, shoot archery, play ping-pong, read a book on the Knoll and get distracted and watch some ants for a while, climb the tower, sail a catamaran, play volleyball, or a round of Frolf. It’s your time, every day, to have free reign of the camp: just get out there, and have fun.
