Craft Areas

Some prefer to make something with their hands whether it be of pine, hemp, or feathers. We can teach you how to do much more than just the traditional arts and crafts, such as fly tying and wood working.

Woodworking

Our well stocked woodworking shop has enough material for anyone to undertake a reasonable project and the expertise to help you along the way in making your wooden masterpiece.

Arts & Crafts

Hemp, necklaces, gimp, kites, and much more are available for anyone to make in the arts and crafts room. But, if stationary crafts are not your cup of tea, you can build your own model rocket and launch it far above the trees!
