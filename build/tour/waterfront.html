Waterfront

Our Waterfront has a half-mile of frontage on quiet Lower Beech Pond. We have a fleet of sailboats, canoes, kayaks and rowboats, a competition water-ski boat, a top-quality swimming area, fishing equipment and a volleyball court for those taking a break from the water.
