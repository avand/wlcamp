Farmhouse

The Farmhouse is home to the Camp's year round administrative offices in addition to the Library, Recreation room, Arts and Crafts, Camp Store, and senior staff housing. The structure itself dates back to the original owner of the land, the farming family Copp, thus is a building rich with tradition and character. Our Library houses plaques which catalogue past campers and staff members, as well as those who have earned the right to be called a Brave.
